# Research Note Template

Use this template for `workflow_auto_assembler` research notes. The exact content under each section can change, but the outer structure should stay stable across notes.

Use the `##` sections as the stable outer structure. The `###` subsections are recommended defaults, but they are optional and can be omitted, merged, or adjusted when a note does not need that level of detail.
